    Who we are!

    We are a team of professional instructors with experience in the usage of the English speech sounds. We use the sounds of the IPA – International Phonetic Alphabet – to dance around the actualization of our purpose in schools.

    We train students and teachers using phonics, phonetics and diction in order to actualize excellent spoken English of the British-American standard, improved realizing skills and encourage a better spelling pattern obtainable in the international community.

    About us

    Our Mission!

    To communicate qualitative English account, pronunciation and excellent transcription skills which enhances pupils/students and teachers knowledge of the IPA – International Phonetics Alphabets – hereby realizing the perfect standard of proficiency in fluency.

    Our Vision!

    We desire to see pupils/students and teachers fully maximized their potentials as far as communication and eloquence is concerned even as they excel in other activities

    Our Core Values!

    --Graceful pronunciation and fluency both in reading and speaking.
    --Outstanding spelling and transcription skills.
    --Mastery of both English and local dialects.
    --Efficient and eloquent communication skills.

    Diction Empire International presents a 3-days bootcamp for teens and young adults.

    Theme: Effective communication

    Date: Thur 5th June 2022 - Sat 7th June 2022

    Venue: Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit.

    Fee: N25,000

    This boot camp will provide a unique and exciting way of exposing participants to core personal development concepts as well as success skills that are required in the present day communication. The boot camp will also provide a forum for participants from various backgrounds and schools to meet, interact and share ideas and experiences in a way that fosters human integration.

    At the end of this boot camp, we promise that participants would be able to understand better the difference between formal and informal writing, appreciate the negative effects of social media slangs, recognize areas for improvement for effective communication, use and interpret body langua effective communication, conduct effective research using proper sources and hold conversations and influence people.

    Boot Camp Agenda:

    • Non-verbal communication skills practice.
    • Elements of Effective Communication: Speaking skills, questioning skills, listening skills and research skills.
    • Barriers to Communication.
    • Verbal communication skills practice.
